My Jigsaw Puzzle

An Android app that lets you create and solve jigsaw puzzles using photos saved on your device.
Your progress is saved automatically, so you can pick up right where you left off.

Features

  • Use your own photos as puzzle images
  • Move pieces with drag, zoom, and pan gestures
  • Choose the number of pieces to adjust difficulty
  • Progress is saved automatically — resume any time
  • Customize the background color
  • Works entirely on your device — no internet connection needed
