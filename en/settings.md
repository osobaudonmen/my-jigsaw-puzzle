## Settings

### Settings Panel (Game Screen)

- **Piece count**: Change the number of pieces. Changing this resets the current piece layout.
- **Background color**: Change the background color of the play area.
- **Delete puzzle**: Delete this puzzle. This cannot be undone.
- **Reset game**: Restart the puzzle from scratch. A confirmation is shown before it takes effect.

### Notes

- Changing the piece count resets your current progress.
- Both reset and delete require confirmation before they are applied.
