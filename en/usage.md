## How to Play

1. On the puzzle list screen, tap `+` to select a photo and create a puzzle.
2. Tap a puzzle from the list to open the game screen.
3. Move pieces in the play area to complete the puzzle.

## Controls

- **Drag**: Move a piece
- **Pinch**: Zoom in or out
- **Drag on empty area**: Pan the view (drag where there are no pieces)
- **Settings panel**: Change piece count, background color, or reset the game

## Pause and Resume

Your progress is saved automatically when you leave the game screen.
The next time you open the same puzzle, the piece positions and zoom level are restored exactly as you left them.
