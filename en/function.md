## Features

### Puzzle List

Create and manage puzzles using photos saved on your device.

- Select a photo to create a puzzle
- View your puzzles and check your progress
- Delete a puzzle along with its photo

### Game Screen

Move pieces with intuitive touch gestures to complete the puzzle.

- Drag pieces to move them
- Pinch to zoom in or out
- Drag on an empty area to pan the view
- Show or hide the settings panel
- Check completion progress
- Reset the game

### Number of Pieces

Adjust difficulty by choosing the number of pieces.

Choose from: 10 / 30 / 50 / 100 / 300 / 500 / 1000

Available options are automatically adjusted based on the image size.

### Pause and Resume

Your progress is saved automatically — pick up right where you left off.

What is saved:

- Piece positions
- View position
- Zoom level
